# Definition
- Specs are the basic attributes of a cybertronian.
- Specs will generally remain the same regardless of the state of transformation.
## Allocation
- **1d4**
	- Poor
- **1d6**
	- Typical
- **1d8**
	- Excellent
- **1d10**
	- Remarkable
- **1d12**
	- Incredible
## Specs
### Strength
- Strength is the measure of athletic prowess, stamina, durability, and physicality. 
### Speed
- Speed is the measure of agility, coordination, manual dexterity, and fine motor skills.
### Smarts
- Smarts are the measure of your intellect, memorization ability, logic, and problem solving.
### Social
- Social is the measure of your charm, charisma, and wit.
### Spark
- Spark is the measure of your willpower, vitality, morality, and resilience.
