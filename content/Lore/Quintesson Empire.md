 The [[Quintesson Empire]] is an extremely advanced faction made up of the [[Quintessons]] and their conquered worlds. The [[Quintessons]] are a species of extremely small and frail squid-like creatures, they hail from the planet [[Quintessa]] after [[Quintis prime]] had created them with the [[Quintis prime#Emberstone|Emberstone]] and inherited his intellect. They began conquering other worlds shortly after they developed intergalactic space travel, the empire quickly expanded due to the [[Quintessons]] extreme intelligence.
