The 13 Primes were created by [[Primus]] as a way to combat [[Unicron]] after their eons of battle remaining in a stalemate. The 13 were modeled based on traits from both [[Primus]] and [[Unicron]] which helped immensely with defeating [[Unicron]] but ultimately lead to their downfall.

The 13 Primes successfully completed their mission of defeating [[Unicron]], but not without great cost. All 13 of them were touched by [[Unicron]]s corruption which eventually lead to the deaths of [[Solus Prime]] and [[Liege Maximo]]. 

Some of the Primes stayed behind on Cybertron and eventually created the [[Cybertronian]]s in their image.
