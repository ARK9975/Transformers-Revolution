The [[Rust Plague]] is a microorganism that corrodes and devours [[Cybertronian]] metals. The corrosion eats away at the metal until there is nothing left. Any attempts to study or contain the disease have resulted in hundreds of deaths and medical facilities rusting into oblivion.  No cure was ever developed and the only defense against this disease is not being infected.
