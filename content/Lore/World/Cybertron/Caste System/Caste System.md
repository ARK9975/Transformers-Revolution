The [[Caste System]] otherwise known as Functionism is a system designed to rigidly define a social and political hierarchy on [[Cybertron]]. It has been implemented into and ripped out of [[Cybertronian]] society since the [[Age of Wrath]].

Newly emerging [[Cybertronian]]s would be harvested from the [[Well of Allsparks]], given a numbered designation, and immediately assigned a job based on their natural [[Alternate Mode]], their job would determine their caste. The system was designed in such a way that no [[Cybertronian]] could change or escape their job and cast.
