The [[Geosynchronus Energon Bridge]] was built by [[Shockwave]] in [[Crystal City]] as a means of directly beaming [[Energon]] to remote orbital installations like the [[Moonbases]], [[Trypticon Station]], and the [[Spacebridge]]s.
