Energon is a naturally occurring fuel source found in a crystal (unprocessed) or liquid (processed) state found on [[Cybertron]], it is typically stored in the form of Energon Cubes but it can be stored in other containers. It is the lifeblood, fuel, ammunition, and general source of energy for [[Cybertronian]]s and their technology (think of it like water, food, blood, and gasoline all in one). Energon must be processed in order for a [[Cybertronian]] to consume it.
