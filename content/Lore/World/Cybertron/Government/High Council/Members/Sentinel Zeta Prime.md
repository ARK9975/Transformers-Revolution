> [!info] Current Appearance
> ![[WFC_ZetaPrime.jpg]]
> [[Sentinel Zeta Prime]]'s current appearance reflects his position as the high general of [[Cybertron]]. His colour scheme remains the same blue with gold and red highlights it has always been but with more gold highlights and his [[Bot Mode]] appearing more ornate.

[[Sentinel Zeta Prime]] is the current leader of [[Cybertron]] being the head of the [[High Council]]. He was used as a puppet just like the rest of the [[High Council]] during the [[Quintesson Empire]]'s [[Age of Wrath]], however he was the primary driving force in forming the resistance force and lead them to victory against the tyrannical [[Quintesson Empire]].
