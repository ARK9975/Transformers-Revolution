[[Halogen]] is the longest serving member of the [[High Council]], rumors say he personally knew some of the [[13 Primes]]. He helped lead [[Cybertron]] through every majour era, but not without great cost. [[Halogen]] became spiteful over the cycle of violence [[Cybertron]] seemed to be stuck in and eventually gave into his greed. His district includes most of the less desirable but extremely profitable regions of [[Cybertron]] like [[Kaon]] and [[Blaster City]], though he desired to annex the [[Hydrax Plateau]] and expand his manufacturing.
