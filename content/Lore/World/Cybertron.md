Cybertron is the heart of the [[Cybertronian Empire]] and the home planet of the [[Cybertronian]]s, it is a massive shining metal, technological world. It's natural landscapes of vast metallic planes, neon bodies of [[Energon]], spiraling glistening mountains, and neon lit caverns have mostly given way to the sprawling cities of the crumbling [[Cybertronian Empire]]. An ancient [[Caste System]] has been in place for millennia resulting in the creation of factory cities like [[Kaon]], [[Energon]] no longer flowing from the core, and Cybertron itself dying.

There is a legend about [[Primus]] being the core of Cybertron...
