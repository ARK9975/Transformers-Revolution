Cybertron is the heart of the [[Cybertronian Empire]] and the home planet of the [[Cybertronian]]s, it is a massive shining metal, technological world. It's natural landscapes of vast metallic planes, neon bodies of [[Energon]], spiraling glistening mountains, and neon lit caverns have mostly given way to the sprawling cities of the crumbling [[Cybertronian Empire]].
