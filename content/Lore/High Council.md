The [[High Council]] has acted as the leaders of [[Cybertron]] throughout history. It was structured to mimic the [[13 Primes]] with 13 members and convening at the [[High Council]] Tower in [[Iacon]]. They played a key part in ending the [[Age of Wrath]] despite being puppets of the [[Quintesson Empire]]. 

The [[High Council]] gradually grew lazy and complacent as [[Cybertron]] flourished during the [[Golden Age]] which contributed to their lackluster response to the [[Rust Plague]], leading to the collapse of the [[Cybertronian Empire]] and the [[Age of Rust]].
