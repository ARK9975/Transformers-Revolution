The [[Age of Rust]] famously began with the halt of expansion and a mysterious [[Rust Plague]] began wiping out remote [[Cybertronian Empire]] colonies. News of the new [[Rust Plague]] had reached [[Cybertron]] shortly after a dozen colonies went offline, this caused a mass panic among the other colonies and on [[Cybertron]]. The [[High Council]] made the hasty decision to isolate [[Cybertron]] from the outer worlds in an effort to prevent the lethal disease from reaching the core of the [[Cybertronian Empire]]. 

It wasn't long before all communications to the outside colonies were lost and the [[Spacebridge]]s fell into disarray due to neglect; the [[Cybertronian Empire]] had effectively crumbled. The cracks in the reformed [[Cybertronian]] society began to show as the great [[Energon]] droughts got worse and the production of new [[Cybertronian]]s reached an all time low. The desperate circumstances lead the [[High Council]] to approve [[Sentinel Zeta Prime]]'s motion for emergency wartime powers, this allowed him to rule [[Cybertron]] with an iron fist in the name of the greater good.

[[Sentinel Zeta Prime]]'s first act as the [[Supreme General]] of [[Cybertron]] was to reinstate the [[Caste System]] in order to better ration [[Energon]] and keep the society from totally crumbling like the [[Cybertronian Empire]]. The [[Well of Allsparks]] also returned to it's unnatural behaviour of producing nameless [[Cybertronian]]s just as it had during the [[Age of Wrath]].
