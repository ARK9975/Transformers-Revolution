The [[Age of Wrath]] began when the [[Quintesson Empire]] first made contact with [[Cybertron]], initially they presented themselves as benevolent beings who sought to elevate [[Cybertron]] by accelerating their societal, economic, and technological development. The [[Quintesson Empire]] established a global government with the [[High Council]] acting as the leaders, however they were simply puppets of the [[Quintesson Empire]]. 

The [[Quintesson Empire]]'s true goal was to strip the individuality out of the [[Cybertronian]]s and create the perfect slaves. They went as far as tampering with the [[Well of Allsparks]] resulting in new [[Cybertronian]]s being born with alphanumeric designations rather than names, new unnatural types of [[Cybertronian]]s like the [[Insecticon]]s, and a slowing [[Energon]] output.

The [[Age of Wrath]] ended with the resistance force formed by the [[High Council]] and lead by [[Sentinel Zeta Prime]], this marked the beginning of the [[Golden Age]] and the [[Cybertronian Empire]].
