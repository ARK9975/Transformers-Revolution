The [[Golden Age]] began with the end of the tyrannical rule of the [[Quintesson Empire]],  officially ending the [[Age of Wrath]]. The new [[Cybertron]] government remained under the rule of the [[High Council]] with [[Sentinel Zeta Prime]] and worked to abolish the [[Caste System]].

This was also a period of rapid expansion for [[Cybertron]]; the [[Well of Allsparks]] produced countless [[Cybertronian]]s with their names and personalities intact, the reverse-engineering of [[Spacebridge]] technology unlocked intergalactic travel, and [[Cyberforming]] technology turned lifeless asteroids into habitable colonies. All of these developments significantly increased the demand for [[Energon]] in remote lead to the creation of the [[Geosynchronus Energon Bridge]] by Chief Science Officer [[Shockwave]].

The [[Cybertronian Empire]] rapidly grew it's colonies with the help of the [[Geosynchronus Energon Bridge]], [[Spacebridge]]s, and [[Cyberforming]] technology leading to an unprecedented age of prosperity for [[Cybertron]].

The [[Cybertronian Empire]] continued it's rapid expansion for millennia, until the [[Well of Allsparks]] diminishing output of new [[Cybertronian]]s had become enough of a problem to halt the expansion. Along with this came a great [[Energon]] drought and the [[Rust Plague]]. These events mark the end of the [[Golden Age]] and the beginning of the [[Age of Rust]].
